You fold torn paper<br>
Cut corners while I kiss<br>
Pins to split my lips.<br>
Blood always tastes<br>
Better when it drips<br>
Trickling to amber<br>
Tunnel of trees<br>
The memories and<br>
Secrets we trap<br>
Beneath bleeding leaves<br>
Turns to sap and grief.<br>
